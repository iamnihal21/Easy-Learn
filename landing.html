What is Pomodoro Clock?

The Pomodoro technique is a simple yet effective tool for focused work with planned breaks in between.

  1. Choose your work to do
  2. Set the timer to 25 minutes
  3. Work until the timer rings
  4. Take a five-minute break
  5. Take longer breaks (15 to 30 minutes) for every four pomodoro intervals
